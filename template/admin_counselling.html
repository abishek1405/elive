ProBono

Because every student with potential deserves a chance — not just those who can afford it.

At Elite Ivy Admissions, we believe in educational equity. While we offer premium, personalized services for students around the world, we also dedicate a portion of our time and resources each year to pro bono admissions support for exceptional students from underprivileged backgrounds.

We know that brilliance is not bound by circumstance — and we're committed to giving deserving students the tools, strategy, and mentorship needed to unlock doors at top U.S. universities.

  • Our pro bono program is highly selective and designed for students who:
    • Come from low-income or disadvantaged backgrounds.
    • Demonstrate outstanding academic achievement or unique personal resilience.
    • Are applying to undergraduate or graduate programs in the U.S.
    • Lack access to quality admissions guidance.

What We Offer (Fully Free of Charge)

✅ Strategic school selection.

✅ Essay guidance and editing.

✅ Resume review and development.

✅ Application filing support.

✅ Scholarship and financial aid application assistance.

✅ Interview prep (including college/visa interviews).

✅ Post-admission guidance.

How to Apply

  • To be considered for our pro bono support, applicants must:
    1. Submit a short application including academic records and personal background.
    2. Provide a statement of need and intent.
    3. Demonstrate high motivation and willingness to commit to the process.
